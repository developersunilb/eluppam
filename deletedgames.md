This file records the ideas for games that have been removed from the project for the initial release. These ideas can be revisited for future updates or for a more advanced version of the app.

## Deleted Games

### Dialect Detective
*   **Description:** A game where players listen to audio clips of Malayalam spoken in different regional dialects and have to identify the region or the meaning of a dialect-specific word.
*   **Benefit:** Exposes players to the rich diversity of Malayalam dialects, improving listening comprehension and cultural understanding.

### Rhythm Rhyme Drummer
*   **Description:** Players have to tap out rhythms on a virtual drum that match the syllabic structure of Malayalam words or phrases.
*   **Benefit:** Helps players internalize the rhythmic patterns and prosody of Malayalam, improving pronunciation and fluency.

### Malayalam Mad Libs
*   **Description:** A classic Mad Libs game where players fill in the blanks with different parts of speech (nouns, verbs, adjectives) to create funny and nonsensical stories in Malayalam.
*   **Benefit:** Reinforces understanding of Malayalam grammar and parts of speech in a fun and creative way.

### Translation Relay Race
*   **Description:** A timed game where players have to translate a series of words or sentences from English to Malayalam (or vice-versa) as quickly as possible.
*   **Benefit:** Improves translation speed and accuracy, and reinforces vocabulary and sentence structure.

### Word Chain Challenge
*   **Description:** Players have to create a chain of Malayalam words where each new word begins with the last letter of the previous word.
*   **Benefit:** A fun way to practice vocabulary recall and spelling.

### Choose Your Own Adventure Story
*   **Description:** An interactive story where players make choices that affect the outcome, with all the text and choices in Malayalam.
*   **Benefit:** Improves reading comprehension and vocabulary in a highly engaging and immersive way.

### Recipe Master Chef
*   **Description:** Players have to follow a recipe written in Malayalam to create a virtual dish, selecting the correct ingredients and following the instructions in order.
*   **Benefit:** Teaches food-related vocabulary and imperative verb forms in a practical and culturally relevant context.

### Treasure Map Navigator
*   **Description:** Players have to follow directions written in Malayalam on a treasure map to find a hidden treasure.
*   **Benefit:** Teaches spatial prepositions and location-based vocabulary.

### Room Label Scanner
*   **Description:** A game that uses the device's camera to "scan" objects in the player's room, and then provides the Malayalam word for that object.
*   **Benefit:** A fun and interactive way to learn the names of common household objects.

### Virtual Onam Market
*   **Description:** A simulation game where players have to buy items for the Onam festival from a virtual market, interacting with vendors in Malayalam.
*   **Benefit:** Teaches vocabulary related to shopping, festivals, and cultural items.

### Mystery Detective Game
*   **Description:** Players have to solve a mystery by reading clues and interviewing virtual characters in Malayalam.
*   **Benefit:** Improves reading comprehension and deductive reasoning skills in Malayalam.

### Kathakali Face Painting
*   **Description:** A creative game where players can paint a virtual Kathakali dancer's face, learning the names of the colors and different parts of the face in Malayalam.
*   **Benefit:** Teaches colors and face-related vocabulary in a culturally rich context.

### Word Auction House
*   **Description:** A multiplayer game where players bid on Malayalam words based on their definition or a clue.
*   **Benefit:** A competitive and fun way to test vocabulary and comprehension.

### Malayalam Pictionary
*   **Description:** A classic Pictionary game where one player draws a word and others have to guess it in Malayalam.
*   **Benefit:** A fun and social way to practice vocabulary and communication skills.

### Boat Race Commentator
*   **Description:** A game where players have to provide commentary for a virtual snake boat race in Malayalam, using appropriate vocabulary and enthusiastic language.
*   **Benefit:** Improves fluency, public speaking confidence, and vocabulary related to sports and events.
	
### Whisper Chain Challenge
*   **Description:** A virtual version of the "telephone" game where a Malayalam phrase is passed from one player to another, with each player having to repeat what they heard.
*   **Benefit:** A fun way to practice listening comprehension and pronunciation.

### Comic Strip Creator
*   **Description:** Players can create their own comic strips using a set of characters and backgrounds, writing the dialogue in Malayalam.
*   **Benefit:** Encourages creative writing and storytelling in Malayalam.

### Puppet Show Theater
*   **Description:** Players can create and perform their own puppet shows, writing the script and dialogue in Malayalam.
*   **Benefit:** Encourages creative writing, storytelling, and performance skills in Malayalam.
